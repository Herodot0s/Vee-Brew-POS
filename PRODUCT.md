# Product

## Register

product

## Users
POS operators (baristas, cashiers, servers) taking orders in a busy, high-throughput coffee shop and brewery environment. They need high speed, zero latency, clear order verification, visual cues for categories and modifiers, and a design that reduces eye strain over long shifts.

## Product Purpose
Vee-Brew-POS is a dedicated POS terminal application that allows staff to take customer orders, configure item modifiers (milk choices, espresso shots, sweet levels), manage order tickets, and process payments. It aims to streamline checkout operations with an interactive, responsive UI.

## Brand Personality
Inspired by Binance's dark trading theme. It is high-contrast, authoritative-financial, tech-forward, and high-voltage, using deep near-black canvases paired with vibrant Binance Yellow (#FCD535) accents for active states and critical calls to action. It treats order-taking with the same precision and urgency as high-frequency trading.

## Anti-references
- Soft, low-contrast pastel designs typical of consumer iPads.
- Light-themed, heavy-whitespace layouts that cause high eye strain over long shifts.
- Sluggish web-view containers with laggy transitions.
- Ambiguous icon-only buttons without text labels.

## Design Principles
1. **High-Voltage Focus**: Reserve vibrant Binance Yellow exclusively for primary checkout actions, selected items, and critical focus indicators to guide the operator's eye instantly.
2. **Tabular Numerical Precision**: Use monospace fonts for all prices, counts, and currency sums to ensure alignment and readability at a glance.
3. **Flat Depth Separation**: Avoid drop shadows, mesh gradients, or glassmorphism. Rely strictly on flat color-block contrast (Canvas Dark vs. Surface Card Dark) for clean, high-performance UI structure.
4. **Ergonomic Density**: Maintain a compact, dense layout to minimize touch target distances and keep all essential controls (category sidebar, product grid, current ticket) visible simultaneously without screen transitions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High-contrast text compliance (all text meeting WCAG AA ratio standards of 4.5:1 against their backgrounds).
- Large touch targets (minimum 44x44px for primary buttons) to accommodate fast, imperfect taps.
- Dark mode by default to mitigate operator eye fatigue during extended screen use.
- Accessible focus indicators for keyboard navigation.
